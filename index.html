My Journey

At 24, my path has been anything but ordinary. Originally from the beautiful island of Saint Lucia, I dedicated years to serving my community as a police officer, where I honed skills in problem-solving under pressure, meticulous attention to detail, and unwavering discipline.

As a certified personal trainer, I learned to motivate others, understand their goals, and create structured plans for success. This focus on human-centered results is something I'm passionate about bringing to the tech industry.

Now, as a Computer Science student at Humber College, I'm channeling that same dedication into learning how to build robust, user-friendly applications. I believe my unique background gives me a different perspective on creating technology that truly serves people.
